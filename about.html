ABOUT SQUID GAME REWIND

Squid Game – College Edition is a thrilling, high-energy competition inspired by the globally popular Netflix series, thoughtfully adapted to ensure a safe, fair, and enjoyable college experience.

Organized by the MU-IEEE Student Branch in collaboration with Techno Clubs, this event brings together students and faculty for an immersive, real-life gaming experience.

The challenges are designed to test not only physical skills but also mental sharpness, teamwork, and decision-making under pressure — all within a fun and competitive atmosphere.
